About the Hall of Fame

The Archbishop Prendergast High School Hall of Fame was established in 1994 to honor distinguished alumnae who have demonstrated excellence in their professional and personal endeavors, contributing to the betterment of the school, community, and society.

We also recognize outstanding members of our community—faculty, staff, parents, and friends—who have made significant contributions to APHS and its students. Together, we seek to inspire current students with strong examples of the benefits of a Prendie education.
